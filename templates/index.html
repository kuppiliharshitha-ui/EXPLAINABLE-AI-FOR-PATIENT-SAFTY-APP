Core capabilities
Everything a clinical AI system needs to be trustworthy.
01
SHAP Explanations
Every prediction comes with SHAP attribution values showing exactly which clinical features drove the risk score — and by how much.
02
Real-time Risk Scoring
Analyzes vitals, labs, and medical history to generate live risk scores for sepsis, deterioration, and clinical complications.
03
Clinical Alerts
Automatically flags abnormal parameters — SpO₂, tachycardia, hypotension, elevated lactate — with severity-graded alerts.
04
Counterfactual Reasoning
Tells clinicians what would need to change to reduce risk — actionable, plain-language explanations linked to clinical parameters.
05
Batch Patient Analysis
Upload ward-level patient lists and receive prioritized risk rankings for entire wards — ideal for morning rounds and triage.
06
Audit Trail & Compliance
Every prediction is logged with full model transparency — supporting HIPAA-compliant documentation and regulatory audit requirements.
How it works
From raw data to explainable insight in seconds.
Step 01
Ingest patient data
Enter vitals, lab results, medication count, and clinical history — or connect directly to your EHR system via REST API.
Step 02
AI risk prediction
A Gradient Boosting model trained on clinical data generates a risk probability score for each patient in milliseconds.
Step 03
SHAP explanation
TreeExplainer decomposes the prediction, attributing positive or negative influence to each clinical feature with mathematical precision.
Step 04
Clinician action
Risk alerts, ranked drivers, and counterfactual suggestions are presented to the clinician to support — not replace — their decision.
